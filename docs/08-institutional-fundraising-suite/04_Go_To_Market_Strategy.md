# Sovereign eCommerce — Go-To-Market Strategy

## ICP and Commercial Focus

- **Primary Market**: D2C brands ($500K-$20M online revenue) selling physical consumer goods in North America
- **Initial Categories**: Beauty/skincare, health/wellness, food/beverage, pet care, apparel, home goods
- **Buyer**: Founder/CEO (decision maker), Head of eCommerce (champion), Head of Marketing (influencer)
- **Trigger Events**: Shopify app costs exceeding platform cost, conversion rate plateau, desire for subscriptions, replatforming from WooCommerce, new D2C brand launch

## GTM Motion: Product-Led Growth with Content Moat

### Pillar 1: Self-Service Product-Led Growth (70% of merchants)

**Free Trial Funnel**:
```
Content / SEO / Referral → Landing Page → 14-Day Free Trial
    → Guided Onboarding (product import, theme selection, payment setup)
    → First Order Within 48 Hours → Trial-to-Paid Conversion
    → Feature Adoption → Tier Upgrade → Advocacy
```

**Onboarding Optimization**:
- Step 1 (5 min): Create account, choose industry, select theme template
- Step 2 (15 min): Import products (CSV, Shopify migration tool, or manual)
- Step 3 (10 min): Configure payment (Stripe Connect one-click), shipping zones, tax settings
- Step 4 (5 min): Connect custom domain (or use free subdomain)
- Step 5: Store is live and ready for first sale
- Target: 78% of trial merchants reach "first order" within 14-day trial

**Conversion Triggers**:
- Trial expiry with "save your store" urgency
- First sale celebration with "upgrade to keep selling" prompt
- Feature gates: abandoned cart recovery requires Growth tier (shown after first abandoned cart)
- Milestone emails: "You've processed $1,000 in orders — here's how to 10x with subscriptions"

### Pillar 2: Content and Community (Brand Building)

**Content Strategy**:
- **D2C Playbook Blog**: 4 articles/week covering subscription strategy, conversion optimization, email marketing, product launch tactics — targeting D2C founders and marketers
- **Benchmark Reports**: Quarterly "State of D2C Commerce" report with anonymized data from platform (conversion rates by category, subscription metrics, AOV trends)
- **Video Content**: YouTube channel with merchant success stories, platform tutorials, D2C strategy interviews (target: 25K subscribers by Year 2)
- **Podcast**: "Building Direct" — weekly podcast interviewing D2C founders on growth strategies

**Community**:
- Discord community (3,200 members): channels for conversion tips, subscription strategy, design feedback, platform support
- Monthly virtual meetup with guest D2C founders
- Annual D2C Summit (virtual, Year 1; in-person, Year 2)

**SEO Targets**:
- "Best Shopify alternative" — Currently rank page 3, target: page 1 position 3
- "D2C ecommerce platform" — Target: page 1 position 5
- "Subscription ecommerce platform" — Target: page 1 position 2
- "Headless commerce for D2C" — Target: page 1 position 1
- Long-tail: 500+ ranking pages for D2C strategy content driving 80K organic visits/month

### Pillar 3: Sales-Assisted Growth (20% of merchants)

**Target**: D2C brands at $5M-$100M annual revenue who need hands-on migration support and custom integration discussions.

**Sales Motion**:
1. Inbound qualification: merchant requests demo through website or content funnel
2. Discovery call (30 min): understand current platform pain, growth goals, tech stack
3. Live demo (45 min): personalized demo with merchant's products imported into sandbox
4. Migration planning (if Shopify/WooCommerce migration): scope products, customers, orders, redirects
5. Trial with white-glove onboarding: dedicated onboarding specialist for 14-day trial
6. Close: annual contract with Pro tier ($299/month) or custom enterprise pricing

**Sales Team** (Year 2):
- 4 AEs handling $5-20M brands (quota: $180K ARR/AE)
- 2 Enterprise AEs handling $20-100M brands (quota: $300K ARR/AE)
- 3 SDRs generating pipeline for AEs
- 2 Solutions Engineers supporting complex migration and integration discussions

### Pillar 4: Agency Partner Channel (10% of merchants)

**Agency Program**:
- 120 certified design/development agencies building stores on Sovereign eCommerce
- Agency benefits: co-marketing, lead referrals, sandbox accounts, priority support, revenue share (10% of referred merchant's first-year subscription)
- Agency certification: complete training course, build 3 demonstration stores, pass assessment
- Agency directory: searchable listing on sovereign-ecommerce.com/agencies

**Agency Targets**: Web design agencies serving D2C brands (typically 5-20 person shops specializing in eCommerce). Each agency refers 2-5 merchants/quarter.

## Pricing Strategy

### Pricing Philosophy
- **All-inclusive by default**: Features that Shopify charges $300-$1,000/month for (subscriptions, recommendations, A/B testing) are included in Growth and Pro tiers
- **Transparent pricing**: No hidden fees, no per-transaction surprises beyond clearly communicated GMV rate
- **Value demonstration**: Free trial proves value before asking for payment; conversion is a natural outcome

### Price Architecture

| Tier | Monthly Fee | GMV Rate | Included Features | Target Merchant |
|------|-----------|----------|-------------------|-----------------|
| Starter | $29 | 2.0% | Unlimited products, visual builder, standard checkout, basic analytics | New D2C brands ($0-500K revenue) |
| Growth | $99 | 1.5% | + Subscriptions, recommendations, abandoned cart, A/B testing, reviews | Growing brands ($500K-$5M) |
| Pro | $299 | 1.0% | + Advanced personalization, headless API, priority support, custom domain SSL | Scaling brands ($5M-$50M) |
| Enterprise | Custom | 0.5-0.8% | + Dedicated support, custom integrations, SLA, multi-store | Large D2C ($50M+) |
