# ERP-eCommerce Data Room Checklist

## Corporate and Legal

- Cap table, board consents, material agreements, IP assignments

## Financial

- Historical statements, rolling forecast, model assumptions, revenue quality analysis

## Commercial

- Pipeline, conversion analytics, cohort retention, pricing and discount governance

## Product and Technology

- Architecture docs, roadmap, reliability metrics, security controls

## Compliance

- Control matrices, evidence artifacts, incident response, DR test logs

## Governance

- KPI dictionary, board decks, risk register, mitigation tracking
