# ERP-eCommerce Risk and Mitigation Framework

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Score | Mitigation |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| Sales cycle elongation | 4 | 4 | 16 | verticalized GTM + partner leverage |
| CAC underperformance | 3 | 4 | 12 | channel mix and conversion optimization |
| Reliability incidents at scale | 3 | 5 | 15 | SLO enforcement + release gating |
| Security/compliance gaps | 2 | 5 | 10 | control automation + external validation |
| Competitive pricing pressure | 4 | 3 | 12 | value-based packaging and differentiation |

## Governance Controls

- Monthly risk review with owners and mitigation status.
- Trigger-based contingency plans tied to burn, win-rate, and uptime.
- Board risk packet with trend and action closure tracking.
