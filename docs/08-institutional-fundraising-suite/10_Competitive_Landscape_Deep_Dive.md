# ERP-eCommerce Competitive Landscape Deep Dive

## Competitive Classes

1. Horizontal generalist platforms
2. Legacy enterprise suites
3. Point automation vendors

## Positioning Matrix

| Capability | ERP-eCommerce | Horizontal | Legacy | Point Vendor |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Domain depth | Strong | Moderate | Moderate | Strong (narrow) |
| Governance controls | Strong | Weak-Moderate | Strong | Weak |
| Speed to value | Strong | Strong | Weak | Moderate |
| Expansion economics | Strong | Moderate | Moderate | Weak-Moderate |

## Strategic Edge

ERP-eCommerce competes on measurable operating outcomes, institutional control depth, and deployment speed rather than lowest price.
