# Sovereign eCommerce — Model-Based Business Plan

## Strategic Objective

Establish Sovereign eCommerce as the leading commerce platform for D2C brands by delivering an all-inclusive platform that replaces the 8-12 tool tech stack with a single, integrated solution. Achieve $24M ARR by Year 3 with 12,000 active merchants and $9B in platform GMV.

## Value Creation Model

### Revenue Engine Architecture

**Primary Revenue: GMV-Based Fee (45% of revenue by Year 3)**
- Mechanism: 1-2% of gross merchandise value transacted through the platform
- Growth driver: As D2C brands scale their businesses, GMV per merchant grows 20% annually
- Retention driver: Moving GMV off platform means rebuilding storefront, losing customer data, and disrupting subscriptions — high switching cost
- Unit economics: Near-zero marginal cost to process incremental GMV

**Secondary Revenue: Platform Subscription (35% of revenue by Year 3)**
- Tiers:
  - Starter ($29/month): 1 staff account, unlimited products, basic themes, standard checkout
  - Growth ($99/month): 5 staff, subscriptions, recommendations, abandoned cart, A/B testing
  - Pro ($299/month): 15 staff, advanced personalization, priority support, custom domain, API access
- Growth driver: Merchants upgrade as they grow and need advanced features
- Average upgrade cycle: 4 months from Starter to Growth, 8 months from Growth to Pro

**Emerging Revenue: Payment Processing + App Marketplace (20% of revenue by Year 3)**
- Payment markup: 0.3-0.5% + $0.10 per transaction on top of Stripe/PayPal base rates
- App marketplace: 20% revenue share from third-party app developers (themes, integrations, marketing tools)
- Both revenue streams scale proportionally with merchant count and GMV

### Customer Lifecycle Model

```
Discovery (Content/SEO/Referral) → Free Trial (14 days) → First Sale (<48 hrs)
  → Paid Conversion → Feature Adoption (subscriptions, recs, A/B testing)
  → Tier Upgrade (Starter → Growth → Pro) → Advocacy (referral, case study)
```

**Cohort Behavior (observed)**:
- Day 0: Merchant signs up for free trial
- Day 1-3: Imports products (or migrates from Shopify), configures theme, adds payment method
- Day 7-14: Launches storefront, receives first order
- Month 1: Converts to paid (Starter tier)
- Month 3: Activates abandoned cart recovery; sees 5-10% revenue recovery
- Month 4: Upgrades to Growth tier for subscriptions and recommendations
- Month 6: Subscribe-and-save generating 15-25% of recurring revenue
- Month 8: Upgrades to Pro for advanced personalization and API access
- Month 12: Referring other D2C founders; becoming community advocate

### ICP Definition

**Primary ICP**: D2C brands with $500K-$20M annual online revenue
- Product: Physical consumer goods (beauty, health, food, apparel, home, pet)
- Team: Founder + 1-5 team members managing eCommerce
- Current platform: Shopify (60%), WooCommerce (20%), custom/other (20%)
- Pain point: Paying $500-$2,500/month for Shopify + apps, or limited by platform capabilities
- Trigger: Hit growth ceiling on current platform, need subscriptions, tired of app complexity
