2. 125%+ net revenue retention demonstrating merchant expansion
3. 75%+ gross margin proving SaaS-grade economics
4. LTV:CAC ratio >10:1 proving efficient PLG + sales motion
5. 10,000+ active merchants demonstrating platform scale
6. App marketplace with 100+ apps showing ecosystem formation
7. Subscription adoption >40% of merchants proving differentiated value
8. International revenue >10% showing geographic expansion potential
