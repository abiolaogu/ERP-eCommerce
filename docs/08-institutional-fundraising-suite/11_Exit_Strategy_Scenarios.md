# ERP-eCommerce Exit Strategy Scenarios

## Exit Paths

- Strategic acquisition by adjacent platform consolidators
- Late-stage private scaling with growth equity
- IPO pathway if scale and quality metrics converge

## Exit Readiness Priorities

1. Revenue quality and retention durability
2. Margin and cash-flow discipline
3. Governance and compliance maturity
4. Strategic relevance in category consolidation
