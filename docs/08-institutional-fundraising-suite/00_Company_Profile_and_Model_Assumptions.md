# Sovereign eCommerce — Company Profile and Model Assumptions

## Company Overview

- **Legal Name**: Sovereign eCommerce, Inc.
- **Founded**: 2024
- **Headquarters**: New York, NY
- **Entity Type**: Delaware C-Corporation
- **Stage**: Series A
- **Employees**: 28 (16 engineering, 5 product/design, 4 sales/CS, 3 G&A)

## Company Description

Sovereign eCommerce is a headless commerce platform purpose-built for direct-to-consumer (D2C) brands. The platform combines the simplicity of a visual storefront builder with the flexibility of a headless API architecture, enabling brands to launch beautiful, high-converting storefronts in hours while maintaining full control over the customer experience. Native subscription commerce, ML-powered personalization, and conversion optimization tools are built into the core platform — not expensive add-ons.

## Market Positioning

- **Target Market**: D2C brands and multi-channel merchants ($500K-$100M annual online revenue) selling physical consumer products across categories including beauty, health/wellness, food/beverage, apparel, home goods, and pet care
- **TAM**: $8B (global eCommerce platform spend for SMB/mid-market merchants)
- **SAM**: $3.2B (North American D2C brands and digitally-native merchants)
- **SOM**: $160M (initial target: 4,000 merchants at $40K average annual platform value)

## Revenue Model

| Revenue Stream | Description | Pricing |
|---------------|-------------|---------|
| GMV-Based Fee | Percentage of gross merchandise value transacted through platform | 1-2% of GMV (tiered by volume) |
| Platform Subscription | Monthly SaaS fee for platform access and features | $29-$299/month based on tier |
| App/Extension Marketplace | Revenue share from third-party app developers | 20% of app revenue |
| Payment Processing Markup | Spread on payment processing (Stripe/PayPal) | 0.3-0.5% + $0.10 per transaction |

## Model Assumptions

### Revenue Assumptions
- Year 1 merchants: 800 (mix of self-service signups and sales-assisted)
- Average GMV per merchant: $1.2M/year (D2C brand average)
- GMV take rate: 1.4% blended (higher for smaller merchants, lower for large)
- Platform subscription ARPU: $89/month ($1,068/year)
- Net revenue retention: 125% (expansion from GMV growth + tier upgrades)
- Logo churn: 6% monthly for Starter tier, 3% for Growth tier, 1% for Pro tier

### Cost Assumptions
- Engineering compensation: 50% of operating expenses
- Gross margin: 72% (main COGS: cloud infrastructure, payment processing pass-through, CDN costs)
- Customer acquisition cost: $350 for self-service, $2,500 for sales-assisted
- Blended CAC: $800
- LTV:CAC ratio target: 6:1 by Year 2

### Growth Assumptions
- Merchant growth: 3x Year 1-2, 2.5x Year 2-3, 2x Year 3-4, 1.5x Year 4-5
- GMV per merchant grows 20% annually (organic growth from D2C brand scaling)
- Subscription ARPU grows 15% annually as merchants upgrade tiers
- International expansion (UK, EU, ANZ) beginning Year 2 adds 25% to addressable market

### Operational Assumptions
- Self-service onboarding: merchant launches store in <48 hours without human assistance
- Freemium trial: 14-day free trial converting at 8-12% to paid
- Support ratio: 1 support agent per 500 merchants (self-service model)
- Engineering team grows to 45 by end of Year 2
- Fully remote team with optional co-working hubs in NYC and LA
