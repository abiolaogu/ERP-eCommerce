# Business Plan — Sovereign eCommerce

## 1. Company Overview

**Mission**: Democratize headless commerce so every D2C brand can deliver world-class shopping experiences without a dedicated engineering team.

**Founded**: 2024
**Headquarters**: Austin, TX
**Employees**: 34
**Stage**: Series A

Sovereign eCommerce is a managed headless commerce platform serving direct-to-consumer brands with $1M-$100M in annual GMV. We combine composable commerce APIs, an AI-powered storefront builder, native subscription management, and multi-channel orchestration into a platform that is as flexible as commercetools but as easy to adopt as Shopify.

## 2. Problem Statement

### The D2C Commerce Infrastructure Crisis

The direct-to-consumer market has grown from $76B (2019) to $213B (2025), with 2.5M+ D2C brands in the US alone. These brands face escalating commerce infrastructure costs:

| Problem | Impact | Affected Brands |
|---|---|---|
| **High take rates** | Shopify charges 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction + monthly fees. A $10M GMV brand pays $290K+/year. | All Shopify merchants |
| **Template constraints** | Monolithic platforms limit brand expression. Checkout customization is impossible on Shopify without Checkout Extensibility (Plus only). | Brands seeking differentiation |
| **Headless complexity** | True headless platforms (commercetools, Contentful Commerce) require $200K+ investment and 6+ developers. | Brands wanting flexibility |
| **Subscription gaps** | Native subscription support is missing from all major platforms. Third-party apps (ReCharge, Bold) cost $300-$500/month and frequently break during platform updates. | 35% of D2C brands with subscription offerings |
| **Multi-channel fragmentation** | Selling on web + Amazon + Instagram + TikTok + wholesale requires separate inventory systems, manual reconciliation, and 24-48 hour data lag. | Brands selling on 3+ channels |
| **ERP disconnect** | Orders flow into accounting systems via CSV exports and manual entry. Financial close takes 5-10 days instead of real-time. | Brands scaling past $5M GMV |

## 3. Solution

### Platform Architecture

Sovereign eCommerce is a composable commerce platform with six core modules:

| Module | Capabilities | Pricing |
|---|---|---|
| **Commerce Engine** | Product catalog, variants, collections, search, filtering, custom attributes | Included in all tiers |
| **Cart & Checkout** | Headless cart, customizable checkout, payment orchestration (Stripe, PayPal, Afterpay, Klarna), tax calculation | Included in all tiers |
| **Order Management** | Order lifecycle, fulfillment routing, returns/exchanges, split shipments, multi-warehouse | Included in all tiers |
| **Subscription Engine** | Recurring orders, billing cycles, skip/pause/swap, churn prediction, winback automation | Growth tier and above |
| **Multi-Channel Hub** | Web, mobile, Amazon, Walmart, Instagram, TikTok Shop, wholesale — unified inventory and pricing | Growth tier and above |
| **AI Storefront Builder** | Visual editor, AI-generated layouts and copy, A/B testing, personalization | Scale tier and above |

### Key Differentiators

1. **Managed Headless**: Unlike Medusa or Saleor (self-hosted open-source), we handle hosting, scaling, security, and updates. Unlike commercetools, we do not require enterprise budgets or large engineering teams.

2. **AI Storefront Builder**: Our AI generates complete storefronts from brand guidelines and product catalogs. A brand owner can create a custom headless storefront without writing code. This expands our addressable market 5x beyond developer-focused headless platforms.

3. **Native Subscription Commerce**: Built into the platform, not bolted on. Handles complex scenarios: gift subscriptions, multi-product bundles, dynamic frequency, prepaid subscriptions, and subscriber churn prediction. No third-party app dependency.

4. **Composable Adoption**: Merchants can adopt incrementally. Start with checkout only (replace Shopify Checkout), then add catalog, then orders, then subscriptions. This reduces switching friction and allows gradual migration.

5. **Lower Take Rate**: 1-2% GMV fee vs. Shopify's 2.9%+. At $10M GMV, merchants save $100K-$190K/year. This is a permanent structural advantage — our infrastructure costs per transaction are 60% lower than Shopify due to headless architecture and modern payment orchestration.

## 5. Go-To-Market Strategy

### Customer Acquisition Channels

| Channel | Description | CAC | % of New Merchants | Scalability |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Self-serve (PLG)** | Free trial, SEO, content marketing | $800 | 45% | High |
| **Outbound sales** | Target Shopify Plus and BigCommerce merchants | $4,500 | 30% | Medium |
| **Agency partners** | D2C agencies building storefronts | $1,200 | 25% | High |

### PLG Motion
Our self-serve funnel is the primary growth engine:
1. **Awareness**: SEO content ranks for "Shopify alternative," "headless commerce," "D2C platform" (200+ blog posts, 45K monthly organic visitors)
2. **Trial**: Free 14-day trial with Shopify import tool. Merchant has a working store within 4 hours.
3. **Conversion**: 18% trial-to-paid conversion rate (vs. 5-8% SaaS benchmark)
4. **Expansion**: Merchants upgrade tiers as GMV grows. Natural expansion from Starter -> Growth -> Scale.

### Outbound Sales Motion
- **Target**: Shopify Plus merchants paying $2K+/month, BigCommerce merchants at plan limits
- **Message**: "Save 50-70% on platform costs while gaining the flexibility to customize everything"
- **Proof point**: ROI calculator showing 12-month savings. Average Shopify Plus merchant saves $85K/year by switching.
- **Sales cycle**: 2-4 weeks (short for eCommerce platform switches)
- **Current team**: 3 AEs, scaling to 10 with Series A funding

### Agency Partner Program
- **40+ D2C agency partners** who build storefronts and recommend platforms
- **Revenue share**: 15% of first-year subscription for referred merchants
- **Enablement**: Agency partner portal, co-marketing funds, priority support
- **Pipeline**: Each active agency generates 8-12 merchants/year
