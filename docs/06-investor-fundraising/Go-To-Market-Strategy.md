## 3. Pricing Strategy

### Pricing Philosophy
1. **Lower than Shopify at every GMV level**: Our structural cost advantage (headless architecture, efficient payment orchestration) allows us to offer 40-60% lower total platform cost.
2. **Align revenue to merchant success**: GMV-based pricing means we grow as merchants grow. This creates a partnership dynamic, not a vendor-customer dynamic.
3. **Transparent, predictable**: No hidden fees, no transaction penalties, no surprise overages. Merchants can calculate their monthly cost before signing up.
