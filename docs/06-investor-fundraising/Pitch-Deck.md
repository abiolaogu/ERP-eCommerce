## Slide 2: The Problem

D2C brands are trapped between simplicity and control:

| Pain Point | Impact |
|---|---|
| **Shopify tax** | 2.9%+ take rate erodes margins as brands scale. A $10M GMV brand pays $290K+/year in platform fees alone. |
| **Template prison** | Monolithic platforms constrain brand experience. Every Shopify store looks like a Shopify store. |
| **Headless complexity** | commercetools, Contentful, and similar platforms require $200K+ and 6+ developers. Inaccessible to growing brands. |
| **Subscription hacks** | Shopify and BigCommerce lack native subscription support. Third-party apps (ReCharge, Bold) are expensive, unreliable, and break during platform updates. |
| **Multi-channel chaos** | Selling on web + Amazon + Instagram + wholesale requires 4 different inventory systems and manual reconciliation. |
| **ERP disconnect** | Orders flow into QuickBooks or NetSuite via CSV exports and manual entry, creating a 24-48 hour data lag. |

**Result**: D2C brands spend 15-25% of revenue on commerce infrastructure instead of product and marketing.
